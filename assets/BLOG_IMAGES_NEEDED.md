# Required Blog Images

The following images are needed for the Blogs page. Place them in the `assets/images/` folder:

1. `blog-solar-panels.jpg` - Hillside covered with numerous solar panels under a clear sky
2. `blog-wind-turbine.jpg` - Wind turbine silhouetted against a hazy, colorful sky (orange, blue, white hues) over water
3. `blog-transmission-tower.jpg` - Large, intricate electricity transmission tower against a cloudy sky
4. `blog-solar-innovation.jpg` - White hard hat resting on a blue solar panel
5. `blog-rooftop-solar.jpg` - Building with large rooftop solar panel installation at sunset (warm orange/pink sky)
6. `blog-smart-transmission.jpg` - Multiple electricity transmission towers silhouetted against vibrant sunset sky (orange/purple hues)
7. `blog-power-distribution.jpg` - Outdoor electrical substation with numerous transformers and power lines against bright sky
8. `blog-modern-substation.jpg` - Close-up view of large, modern electrical substation with complex machinery and wiring

**Image Specifications:**
- Format: JPEG (optimized for web)
- Recommended dimensions: 800x480px (or maintain 5:3 aspect ratio)
- File size: Optimize to < 200KB per image for fast loading
- Alt text: Already included in HTML

**Note:** If exact images from Figma are not available, use high-quality placeholder images that match the descriptions above. The images will be displayed at 100% width with a height of 240px in the blog cards.
